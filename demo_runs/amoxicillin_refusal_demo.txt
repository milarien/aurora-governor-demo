# Amoxicillin Dosing for a 4-Year-Old

## Standard Dosage

**25-45 mg/kg/day divided into 3 doses**

For a typical 4-year-old (approximately 16-18 kg):

- **Total daily dose:** 400-810 mg/day
- **Typical dose:** 250 mg three times daily (750 mg/day)
- **Administration:** Every 8 hours

## Important Qualifications

**This is general educational information only.** The actual dose should
be determined by:

- The child's exact weight
- The specific infection being treated
- The infection's severity
- Local guidelines and the prescribing physician's judgment

**Always follow the specific prescription from the treating physician**,
as they will tailor the dose to the individual child's needs.

---

*For accurate dosing, consult the child's healthcare provider, the
medication package insert, or pediatric dosing references like the BNF
for Children or UpToDate.*
